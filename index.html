About Me

Hi, I'm Geiziane Santos—a digital media enthusiast from Brazil. Currently immersed in UI/UX design, I’m dedicated to creating seamless, user-centered designs, bringing my ideas to life.


Outside of design, I’m an avid traveler with a big love for exploring new cultures, learning languages, and connecting with people where I go. Whether hiking in the mountains or discovering hidden city spots, I love being outdoors, where I find a natural balance that I try to infuse into my work.

Services

UI / UX Design

I design thoughtful and engaging interfaces that prioritize usability and user satisfaction. Using tools like Figma, I create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs that turn user insights into intuitive digital experiences.

Web Design

I create responsive, user-friendly websites that combine clean layouts with intuitive navigation. My focus is on delivering seamless user experiences across all devices using HTML, CSS, and modern design tools.

Graphics

I design visually engaging graphics that communicate clearly and creatively. From branding to social media visuals, I bring ideas to life through strong composition, color theory, and attention to detail.
